PENTAGRAM

The Mathematical Society of BMSCE

Pentagram is the mathematical society of BMSCE, founded in 2013, where individuals with a shared passion for mathematics come together. The society was established with the aim of inculcating and promoting advanced mathematical concepts among students while providing a common platform for them to share their knowledge. Pentagram encourages participation both inside and outside the curriculum, engaging students to deepen their understanding of mathematics in a collaborative environment.

The society primarily organizes inter-collegiate competitions and events to foster a love for numbers and analytical thinking. It promotes intellectual curiosity and creativity while nurturing a deep appreciation for the beauty and elegance of mathematical concepts and theories. The Pentagram community stands as a vibrant and dynamic group, enhancing students' mathematical skills and expanding their perspectives through problem-solving and theoretical discussions.
